Press Release

“Tufts Game Design Team Announces TEMS vs. Drunk College Student”

“Take on the role of a drunk college student who is trying NOT to get TEMsed!”

On December 18th, a Tufts University Game Design team will release TEMS vs. Drunk College Student as a MAC and PC game. This unique top-down, first person shooter game can help you relive your Tufts college experience.

Had one too many drinks and everything is a blur. You don’t know where your body is taking you and the academic quad looks like a maze. To make things worse, the entire TEMS department is after you. If you get caught, they’ll take you to the hospital against your will and you’ll end up with a fat, juicy medical bill at your front door. Moreover, the last thing you want is your reputation to become “that kid who got TEMsed.”

Place yourself in the shoes of a drunk college student who is trying NOT to get TEMsed! As you navigate around the Tufts academic quad, avoid getting caught by a TEMs officer at all cost! Use your vomit powers to paralyze your opponents and be sure to pick up alcohol bottles for unique power-ups including, super-speed and teleportation!
